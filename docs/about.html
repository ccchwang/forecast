About

Hi, I’m Cho. My full name in Korean means “green”—perfect for a Taurus Sun born in the full bloom of May 🌳

I was raised in a traditional Korean family where my mom and grandma could tell the future from their dreams. Although I don't have their gift, I like to think it’s manifesting in me through astrology—the idea that time is circular, not linear, and that predicting the future is as simple as looking back to the past.

I want to help you understand how your soul's past affects the present, and how the present is a stepping stone towards your future evolution.
In 2020, I’m taking a huge leap to launch my own astrology business, The Astro Forecast. My mission is to help you better understand the incoming astrological weather so that you can move with the winds, not against them. The universe is always supporting us—we just have to know where it's taking us.

I have lots planned and can't wait to share it with you! Find me on Instagram to come along for the ride.
